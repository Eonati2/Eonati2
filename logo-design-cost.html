Every Logo Design Pricing Tier Explained

Here is an honest breakdown of every provider type, what they cost, and what you actually get — including the trade-offs that marketing copy never mentions.

Tier 1
AI Logo Generators (Looka, Tailor Brands, etc.)
€0–€20/month
AI generators produce templated graphics based on your inputs. They are fast and cheap, but the results are non-exclusive, generic, and often not true vector files.
  • Instant results
  • Very low cost
  • Same design may be used by thousands of other businesses
  • Cannot understand your brand positioning or niche
  • IP ownership is often unclear or shared
  • Does not hold up at scale — not true production-grade vectors

Best for: Placeholder logos only. Not recommended for any business that wants to build long-term brand value.

Tier 2
Unvetted Fiverr / Standard Freelance Platforms
€5–€80
Standard (non-Pro) Fiverr sellers are unvetted — anyone can create an account and start selling. Quality varies enormously. At this tier, you may get a copy of an existing logo, low-resolution rasterized files, or non-exclusive designs.
  • No vetting — quality is unpredictable and highly variable
  • Risk of receiving plagiarized or non-original designs
  • Often delivered as raster (PNG/JPG) only — not true vectors
  • Limited recourse if you are unhappy with the result

Best for: Hobbyist projects or very early concept validation only. Not suitable for any commercial brand.

Tier 4
Spec Work Platforms (99designs, DesignCrowd)
€199–€599
Contest-based platforms where multiple designers submit concepts competing for your brief. You pay upfront and receive many options — but the designers are still unvetted, and much of the output will be generic or templated.
  • Multiple concepts to choose from
  • Designers are not vetted — quality is variable
  • Competing designers have no incentive to do their best work on spec
  • Higher cost than Fiverr Pro for comparable or worse quality
Tier 5
Mid-Tier Branding Agencies
€800–€3,000
Mid-tier agencies typically offer a more structured process — discovery sessions, brand strategy, multiple design rounds. In practice, the actual design work is often outsourced to freelancers who are at the same skill level as Fiverr Pro designers.
  • Structured brand strategy process
  • Account management and clear project timelines
  • Design work often subcontracted externally
  • Significantly higher cost for often comparable output
  • Slower — typically 4–8 weeks from brief to delivery
Tier 6
Top-Tier / Enterprise Branding Agencies
€5,000–€50,000+
For enterprise rebrands that include market research, competitor analysis, brand strategy workshops, identity system development, brand guidelines, and multi-stakeholder management. This tier is appropriate for public companies, large consumer brands, and institutions.
  • Complete strategic brand development
  • Full research and positioning work
  • Senior creative talent
  • Significant investment — rarely appropriate below Series B or major rebrands

What Drives the Price Up or Down

Within any tier, these factors affect what you pay:

  • Deliverable scope: A single logo mark costs less than a full brand system with typography, color palette, and usage guide. Know exactly what you need before ordering.
  • Turnaround speed: Express or rush delivery (24 hours) typically costs a premium over standard 3–7 day delivery.
  • Number of revisions: More included revisions generally means a higher base price — but also more flexibility to refine the result.
  • Complexity of the brief: A simple wordmark costs less than a complex icon plus full brand system. Complex packaging with multiple SKUs costs more than a single product label.
  • Exclusivity: All Fiverr Pro deliverables come with exclusive commercial rights by default — unlike spec platforms where designs may have been previously used or submitted to other contests.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I pay for a logo as a small business?
For most small businesses, €23–€112 on Fiverr Pro is the right range. At €23 (Juhi), you get a professional vector logo with full commercial rights. At €112 (Alestra Agency), you get a complete brand system including strategy, typography, and usage guide. Spending more than this is generally not justified until you have significant traction or enterprise clients.
Is a cheap logo design always low quality?
No — price and quality are not directly correlated when you are comparing vetted and unvetted providers. A €23 logo from a Fiverr Pro designer (manually vetted, 9,000+ verified reviews) will almost always outperform a €500 logo from an unvetted freelancer or a generic agency. What matters is the vetting process and the designer's specific expertise in your niche.
What logo files should I receive for my money?
Any professional logo delivery should include: AI or EPS (editable vector for print), SVG (web-optimized scalable vector), PNG with transparent background at multiple sizes, and full commercial usage rights. If a designer does not provide vector files (AI/EPS/SVG), you are not receiving professional-grade deliverables — regardless of the price you paid.
How do I know if my payment is protected?
On Fiverr Pro, all payments are held in escrow. Your money is never transferred to the designer until you review and approve the final work. If you are not satisfied, you can request revisions before approving. This eliminates the payment risk that is common with direct freelancer arrangements or agency retainers.
