Best Logo Design Websites
2025: Ranked and Reviewed

⏱ 8 min read 📅 May 2026 ✍ Eonati Editorial

The logo design industry has fragmented into five distinct categories: AI tools, logo makers, freelancer platforms, contest platforms, and agencies. Each promises professional results. Only some deliver them. This is an honest comparison of every major option — what you actually get, what it costs, and which is right for your situation.

The 6 Types of Logo Design Website Compared

#1 Fiverr Pro
★ Best Overall
Price: From €23
Quality: Vetted professional
Files: AI, EPS, SVG + PNG
Payment: Escrow protected
Fiverr Pro is the manually vetted tier of Fiverr — fewer than 1% of applicants are approved. You get original custom design from a verified professional, full vector source files with commercial rights, and escrow payment that holds your money until you approve every file. The three designers on Eonati have a combined 48,398 completed orders at ★4.8+. This is the strongest value proposition in the market — agency-level output at platform pricing, with better payment protection than either.
Verdict: Best for anyone who needs a professional logo and doesn't want to pay agency prices. The escrow model eliminates risk. The vetting eliminates quality lottery. Start here. Browse Fiverr Pro designers →
#2 99designs
Good but expensive
Price: €299–€1,299
Model: Design contest
Files: Vector included
Payment: Upfront
99designs operates on a contest model — you post a brief and multiple designers submit concepts. You choose the winner. The quality ceiling is higher than standard Fiverr because the incentive structure rewards better work. The problems: it is expensive (minimum €299 for a logo contest), you pay upfront before seeing a single concept, and the contest model wastes designers' time on unpaid speculative work. Vector files and commercial rights are included.
Verdict: A legitimate option for businesses with a higher budget who want multiple concept directions. But at 10x the price of Fiverr Pro entry level, the quality gap does not justify the cost difference for most use cases.
#3 Toptal
Premium quality, premium price
Price: €1,000–€5,000+
Quality: Top 3% of applicants
Files: Full deliverables
Payment: Milestone-based
Toptal's vetting process is genuinely rigorous — approximately 3% acceptance rate — and the quality floor is consistently high. But the pricing model is built around hourly rates and minimum commitments that push logo projects into the €1,000–€5,000 range. For logo design specifically, the output quality is not meaningfully higher than the best Fiverr Pro designers at a fraction of the cost.
Verdict: Right for complex, ongoing design relationships with significant budgets. Not the right tool for a logo project.
#4 Looka / Hatchful / Wix Logo Maker
Avoid for professional use
Price: €10–€80 one-time
Quality: Template-based AI
Files: PNG + some SVG
Design: Shared library
AI logo makers generate visuals from shared template and icon libraries. The output is fast, the price is low, and the result is a logo that is statistically identical to thousands of other businesses using the same platform. These tools produce previews, not professional logos. The icons used are stock elements visible across competitor brands. Most AI logo maker outputs fail the vector source file test — you receive a PNG, not an editable AI/EPS file.
Verdict: Acceptable for a temporary placeholder or internal mock-up. Not acceptable for a real business that needs brand differentiation. You will redesign it within 18 months.
#5 Canva
Not a logo design tool
Price: €13/month (Pro)
Quality: Template drag-and-drop
Files: PNG/PDF (no AI/EPS)
Originality: Shared templates
Canva is a legitimate design tool for social media graphics, presentations, and marketing materials. It is not a logo design tool. Its logo templates are used by millions of businesses globally — your "unique" Canva logo is shared with an uncountable number of competitors. The output cannot be exported as a proper vector source file. Commercial rights are limited to Canva's licence terms, which are not the same as owning the mark outright.
Verdict: Use Canva for everything it is genuinely good at. Do not use it for your logo. The price difference between a Canva Pro subscription and a Fiverr Pro entry logo is smaller than the quality difference is large.
#6 Traditional Design Agency
High quality, high cost
Price: €500–€50,000
Quality: Variable
Strategy: Full brand positioning
Timeline: 4–12 weeks
A full-service branding agency provides strategic brand positioning, research, competitive analysis, and complete visual identity systems. The work is comprehensive and the quality ceiling is high. For logo design specifically, the output quality is comparable to the best Fiverr Pro designers — the price premium buys strategy and account management, not design skill. Agencies are right when the strategic layer has significant commercial value. For visual identity execution, Fiverr Pro competes on quality at a fraction of the cost. Verdict: Right for established enterprises where brand strategy is the primary need. Not necessary for logo design execution alone.
